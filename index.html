Tourism around Australia

Australia has a history of being a tourism goldmine for travellers worldwide. The lucky country offers an abundance of adventures for all types of tourists. Whether you prefer tropical places, surfing hotspots or gastronomically-obsessed cities, Australia has a place to visit. Use this map to explore distribution of tourists amongst its main states. Unfortunately, Tasmania, you have been left out.

State and Regional Tourism

Since 2010, Australian tourist numbers have consistently increased with states like WA gaining consistent tourist interest as reflected with its high growth rates. Use the bump chart to see the rate of tourism growth for the mainland Australian states. Filtering on the chart's legend will retrieve top 5 most visited regions for the selected state. If you notice, main urban hubs of Australian states, that is their capital cities rank consistently as most visited regions.
