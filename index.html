Tourism across Australian Seasons

Being the driest and hottest continent in the world means that Australia's tourism industry is dominated by the warmer months. This is fostered with the abundance of beachtown getaways that stretch across Australia's abundant coastline, particularly with the east coast.

Hover over the heat rectangles to view greater details regarding tourism amongst these quarters. Since 2010, Australia has gained a 30% increase in yearly tourism figures suggesting that tourism is transforming to be a pivotal industry for the country.
