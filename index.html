Tourism in Australia

Upon inspection of the chloropleth map, Northern Territory consistently ranks as the most relatively popular state for tourism, recording values in the high 25 - 30 range. Since 2010, tourism in WA has increased.

Interestingly, the less popular states for tourism face the most volatile swings in growth over the years. Where SA ranked 1 in 2010, it catapulted to the bottom in 2011. In contrast, the stable, popular states remain with a consistent stream of tourism growth.
